# Sephora-Products-EDA
Exploratory data analysis project focused on evaluating factors that contribute to Sephora product success.

## Project Overview
This project explores Sephora product and customer review data to uncover trends in pricing, ratings, and brand performance. The goal was to derive actionable insights that could support business and marketing decisions.

## Objectives
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) on Sephora product and review data.
- Identify trends in product ratings, pricing, and categories.
- Analyze factors influencing customer satisfaction.
- Generate business insights based on data-driven findings.

## Dataset
The dataset includes Sephora product information and customer reviews, such as:
- Product categories and brands
- Pricing information
- Customer ratings and reviews
<br>
Link to dataset:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nadyinky/sephora-products-and-skincare-reviews?select=product_info.csv

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Approach
The project followed a structured data analysis workflow:
1. Select a real-world dataset.
2. Perform data preparation and cleaning.
3. Perform exploratory analysis and visualization.
4. Ask and answer questions about the data.
5. Summarize your inferences and write a conclusion. 

## Key Insights
- Affordable pricing strongly correlates with higher product popularity and broader customer appeal.
- Sephora-exclusive products receive significantly higher engagement, highlighting the impact of exclusivity on consumer interest.
- Niche categories with high customer satisfaction present opportunities for premium positioning and targeted expansion. 

## Conclusion 
This analysis demonstrates how EDA can be used to extract meaningful insights from real-world retail data. The findings highlight the value of data-driven decision-making in understanding customer behavior and product information.
